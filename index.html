Blood Metabolome Dataset in Holstein Cows

Hello!This is a website for sharing blood metabolome data from holstein cattle.

Holstein cattle is a breed of cattle that was crossbred between Chinese scalpers and Holstein cattle introduced into China at the end of the 19th century. Holstein cattle have the characteristics of large size and high milk yield. With the selection and breeding in recent decades, the milk yield, milk fat percentage and milk protein percentage have steadily increased.

We are delighted to have you here! Raw data and clean data from the metabolome of Holstein bovine serum or plasma from two different farms are presented below. If you are interested in our project or have any questions, please feel free to contact us. Thank you!
